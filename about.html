About Us

Welcome to Sahara Cafe, a family-owned and operated restaurant serving delicious food for over 15 years. Our restaurant is located in the heart of Tukwila and offers a cozy and comfortable atmosphere that will make you feel like you are eating at a resturant in somalia.

Our Menu features a variety of dishes made from fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. From classic burgers and fries to exotic seafood, we have something for everyone. We also have a wide selection of different smoothies and pop.

We are dedicated to providing excellent customer service and making every visit to our restaurant a memorable one.

Thank you for choosing Sahara Cafe. We look forward to serving you soon!
